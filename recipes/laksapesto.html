Laksa Pesto Linguine Recipe

The dish that will take your breath away

When it comes to laksa-inspired dishes, the iconic Laksa Pesto Linguine by Mod-Sin pioneer Willin Low remains a firm favourite for many local food lovers. The dish came about rather serendipitously. When chef Willin first opened Wild Rocket, his landlord curiously asked if they would have a laksa on the menu. That sparked something in him, and he decided to come up with a creation that reinvented the classic Singapore dish.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups fresh laksa leaves,packed
  • 1/2 cup freshly grated Parmesan Reggiano cheese
  • 1/2 cup extra virgin olive oil
  • 2 chilli padi
  • 1/3 cup of candle nuts & sun flower seeds
  • 1 bulb garlic cloves,minced
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste
